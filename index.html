Pulse

Your health data, read back to you. Pulse is a private, personal dashboard over your own Google Health / Fitbit data — daily readiness, sleep, heart rate, steps, calories and workouts. It runs on your own device, read-only, and stays with you.

What it does

✓
Reads your own metrics from the Google Health API — activity, heart, sleep, body measurements.
✓
Shows them to you as a calm daily review — no ads, no social, no accounts beyond your own.
✓
Stays private — data is processed on your device and is never sold or shared.
